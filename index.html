VForce Flow

Build it. Click Run.

A visual workflow engine. Drop in a trigger, wire up the steps, branch on a decision — then run it and watch it execute, live. Durable when it matters, simple when it doesn't.

  • Triggers: webhooks, schedules, events, queues
  • Actions, AI agents, drop-in code & compiled functions
  • Branches, loops, approvals, retries, sagas
  • Connect anything — APIs, databases, SaaS, files
  • Runs in your cloud or on-prem, full audit trail
VForce Lakehouse

Your data & documents, one brain.

An open-format lakehouse with intelligent document processing built in. Browse your catalog, query with SQL, and ask in plain English — restricted to the models your org approves.

  • Open tables (Iceberg) + SQL warehouse (Trino)
  • Documents → OCR, classify, extract → queryable rows
  • Catalog you can browse: columns, lineage, history
  • A governed AI analyst that answers with tables & charts
  • Notebooks, dashboards, and fine-grained access

Flow orchestrates. Lakehouse remembers.

Flow runs the pipelines that move, transform, and enrich your data; Lakehouse stores it, governs it, and makes sense of it. A flow can ingest into the lakehouse; the lakehouse can launch flows as pipelines. One loop — automation that feeds a brain, and a brain that triggers automation.

One identity & tenancy

Same governed access across both products — your data never leaves your environment.

Flows-as-code

Everything is versioned in git and compiles to a deployable object any CI/CD can ship.

Own the stack

Open formats, no lock-in. Ships as a Helm chart or a docker-compose bundle.
